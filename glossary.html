TERMINOLOGY // PHL-HQ STANDARDS

Logistics Codex.

The authoritative dictionary of the Everward Global Network protocols and asset classifications.

Mission States

Blueprint

The finalized itinerary encompassing aviation, ground fleet, and stay parameters. Once "Locked," a Blueprint represents a secured logistics commitment.

Deployment

The active phase of a Mission, beginning the moment a Citizen enters a vetted Sector.

Staging

The positioning of ground fleet assets at a terminal or villa 30 minutes prior to a Citizen's scheduled arrival.

Asset Classes

Vetted Sector Asset

A property, aircraft, or vehicle that has passed the 50-point physical security and luxury audit by HQ personnel.

Positioning Rate

The elite hourly rate for moving aviation assets to a specific sector for a Citizen's deployment.

Identity & Security

Global Citizen ID

The unique biometric-linked credential required for all Tier 1 and Tier 2 sector access.

Identity Vault

The 256-bit encrypted personal repository for a Citizen’s logistics data, financial records, and mission history.
