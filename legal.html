01. Terms of Deployment

By initializing a "Mission" or "Search" via the Everward platform, you acknowledge that Global Citizen Joe LLC acts as a primary logistics coordinator. All "Stays," "Aviation," and "Fleet" assets are subject to the individual Sector Governance where they are deployed.

Citizens must maintain a valid ID Vault profile and pass Tier 1 background synchronization before assets are finalized.

02. Data Encryption Policy

We utilize AES-256 bit encryption for all personnel data. Unlike standard retail travel platforms, your PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is never sold to third-party marketing entities. Data is used strictly for Sector Clearance and HQ Coordination.

03. Mission Cancellation Protocols

Missions canceled outside of the 48-hour deployment window are subject to a 15% logistics recovery fee. Missions canceled within the 48-hour window may result in the forfeiture of the "Elite Credit" applied at the time of authorization.
